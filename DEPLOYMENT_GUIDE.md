# 🚀 DEPLOY YOUR PORTFOLIO TO NETLIFY - STEP BY STEP

## ✅ What You Need
- All portfolio files (you have them!)
- A free Netlify account
- 5 minutes

---

## 📋 DEPLOYMENT STEPS

### Step 1: Get Your Files Ready

You already have all files. Just make sure you have this folder structure:

```
portfolio/
├── index.html
├── styles.css
├── data.js
├── script.js
├── netlify.toml
├── README.md
└── assets/
    ├── profile.jpg
    └── Alexander_Agbu_CV.pdf
```

### Step 2: Sign Up for Netlify

1. Go to: **https://netlify.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose **"Sign up with GitHub"** (recommended) or email
4. Follow the quick signup process
5. You're in! You should see your dashboard

### Step 3: Deploy Your Site

**EASIEST METHOD - Drag & Drop:**

1. On the Netlify dashboard, scroll down to **"Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git?"**

2. You'll see a big box that says **"Drag and drop your site folder here"**

3. **Drag your entire portfolio folder** onto that box

4. Wait 20-30 seconds while Netlify builds your site

5. **BOOM! Your site is LIVE! 🎉**

You'll see a URL like: `https://sparkly-unicorn-a1b2c3.netlify.app`

### Step 4: Customize Your URL

1. On the site overview page, click **"Site settings"**

2. Under "Site information", click **"Change site name"**

3. Enter your preferred name (e.g., `alexagbu`)

4. Your site becomes: **`https://alexagbu.netlify.app`**

5. **DONE!** Share this URL on your LinkedIn, CV, everywhere!

## 📝 HOW TO UPDATE YOUR SITE

Whenever you want to add new projects, update experience, add blog posts:

### Option 1: Re-deploy (Drag & Drop)

1. Edit `data.js` on your computer
2. Save the file
3. Go to Netlify → "Deploys" tab
4. Drag the updated folder to the deploy area
5. Done! Changes live in 30 seconds

### Option 2: Use GitHub (Automatic Updates)

If you deployed via GitHub:
1. Edit `data.js` in your repository
2. Commit and push changes
3. Netlify automatically rebuilds your site
4. Done!

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem: Site not loading**
- Solution: Check Netlify deploy logs for errors
- Make sure all files uploaded correctly

**Problem: Images not showing**
- Solution: Verify files are in `assets/` folder
- Check file names match exactly

**Problem: Contact form not working**
- Solution: Forms only work when deployed (not locally)
- Check Netlify dashboard → Forms section

**Problem: Changes not appearing**
- Solution: Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5)
- Wait 1-2 minutes for rebuild

## 🎨 BONUS: Connect Custom Domain (Optional)

Have your own domain (e.g., `www.alexanderagbu.com`)?

1. Go to Netlify → Site settings → Domain management
2. Click "Add custom domain"
3. Enter your domain name
4. Follow the DNS configuration instructions
5. Your site moves to your custom domain!

**Cost:** Netlify hosting is FREE. You only pay for the domain name (~$10-15/year from providers like Namecheap, Google Domains)
