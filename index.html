About Me

I'm a results-oriented Data Scientist with a proven track record of transforming complex data into actionable business intelligence across multiple industries.

My journey in data science began at the National Space Research and Development Agency in Nigeria, where I analyzed satellite imagery for environmental monitoring and urban planning. Since then, I've worked across Real Estate, Blockchain, Energy, and AI sectors, consistently delivering measurable impact.

At Notting Hill Genesis, I built a machine learning model that achieved a 5% reduction in tenant arrears through predictive analytics. I also developed ArrearsRadar, a real-time web application empowering housing managers with data-driven insights.

Beyond technical work, I'm passionate about education. As Lead Data Coach at Codekago, Industry Mentor at Code Your Future, and IT Consultant at a London premier school, I've trained hundreds of aspiring data professionals, helping them bridge the gap between theory and real-world practice.
